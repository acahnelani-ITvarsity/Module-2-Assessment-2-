TRUWORTHS

We strive to: Make the Truworths brand of fashion merchandise the most aspirational, innovative and adventurous blend of colour, fabric, value and fashion styling.

Truworths Limited is a leading South African retailer of fashion merchandise.

The operation has developed a range of specialised retail formats including Truworths Woman, Truworths Man, Daniel Hechter, Inwear and LTD

Truworths is a Cape Town-based, South African clothing retailer, with 728 stores in South Africa, and 49 elsewhere in Africa.
